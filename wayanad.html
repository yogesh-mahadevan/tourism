About Wayanad

Wayanad, located in the northern part of Kerala, is one of the most beautiful hill stations in South India. Known for its lush green landscapes, wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls, and misty mountains, Wayanad offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere for tourists. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast, a nature lover, or someone seeking tranquility, Wayanad has something to offer everyone.

Top Attractions

  • Eddakal Caves - Ancient caves with rock carvings dating back to the Neolithic era, offering a beautiful view of the surroundings.
  • Pookode Lake - A serene freshwater lake surrounded by forests, perfect for boating and picnics.
  • Edakkal Caves - A historical site with petroglyphs and carvings that tell the story of ancient civilizations.
  • Kuruva Island - A protected river delta, famous for its unique ecosystem and lush green forests.
  • Soochipara Waterfalls - A three-tiered waterfall located amidst dense forest, offering a tranquil setting.
