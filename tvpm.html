About Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital of the Indian state of Kerala. The city is known for its historical landmarks, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Located on the southwestern coast of India, Thiruvananthapuram is a hub for tourism, education, and technology. It is home to many historical temples, museums, and picturesque parks.

Top Attractions

  • Shree Padmanabhaswamy Temple - A magnificent temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.
  • Kovalam Beach - A famous beach destination known for its pristine shores and vibrant atmosphere.
  • Napier Museum - A museum showcasing Kerala's rich cultural and artistic heritage.
  • Veli Lake - A serene freshwater lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for picnics and boating.
