About Idukki

Idukki is a picturesque district in Kerala, known for its lush greenery, picturesque hills, and tranquil landscapes. The region is home to several tea plantations, wildlife sanctuaries, and various natural attractions. Located in the Western Ghats, Idukki offers stunning views, waterfalls, and an abundance of wildlife, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Top Attractions

  • Idukki Dam - One of the highest arch dams in Asia, it is a major landmark of the region.
  • Rajamalai (Eravikulam) National Park - A UNESCO World Heritage site, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
  • Cheeyappara Waterfalls - A beautiful multi-tier waterfall, offering a serene setting.
  • Vagamon - A hill station with a cool climate, tea gardens, and meadows.
