About Kasargod

Kasargod, located in the northernmost part of Kerala, is a land rich in culture, history, and natural beauty. Surrounded by stunning beaches, rivers, and hills, Kasargod is a perfect destination for both nature lovers and cultural enthusiasts. Known for its temples, forts, and lush landscapes, Kasargod is a place where tradition meets modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Bekal Fort - A historic fort offering a panoramic view of the Arabian Sea.
  • Kappil Beach - A serene beach ideal for a relaxing day out.
  • Ananthapura Lake Temple - The only lake temple in Kerala, dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
  • Ranipuram - A hill station with lush green hills and a cool climate, perfect for trekking.
